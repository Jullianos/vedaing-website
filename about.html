About us

VEDA is a private production and design enterprise, established in Skopje, in 1991. It is a Research & Development oriented entity, which provides new, custom-made solutions in the field of:

  • Solid fire extinguishing methods
  • Production of biodiesel and energy from renewable sources
  • Thermoplastic composite materials for different applications
  • New materials for insulation with heat resistance of over 3000 C etc

VEDA has a small diverse team of 7 full time employees, most of whom have been with the company since its beginnings. However, due to its commitment to excellence, innovation and state of art technology, VEDA cooperates with an external expert team consisted of experienced mechanical, chemical and electronic engineers, some with over 35 years of experience in their field of expertise.

Since its beginning, VEDA’s vision was production of special processing equipment and products that would meet market demands in particular fields, but at the same time provide solution to some of the most current issues, such as environmental protection. This is why most of its innovative thinking is applied in solutions which take fully into account the environmental protection as an urgent matter in any scientific field.

At the time of the company’s establishing, the Macedonian market was too narrow for VEDA’s ideas, which immediately turned VEDA towards the world market.Thus very soon, the VEDA’s expert team, based on prior knowledge in the field of composite materials and technological processes, successfully created own unique production program, which offered wide application possibilities of its final products. VEDA’s potential partners are now able to utilize its original technological lines for different applications, such as:

  • For innovative solutions in fire extinguishing technology
  • As a new wave of thermoplastic composite materials
  • For advanced insulation solutions and new materials
  • For energy production from renewable resources etc.
The R&D facilities & capacity

The above mentioned was accomplished due to significant and continuous investment in own Research & Development facilities. VEDA’s equipment is designed, assembled and produced according to own documentation and utilizes a computerized system for management of the technological processes with a software also developed by the company itself. Furthermore, VEDA built its own R&D laboratory, technological line with a semi-industrial production capacity as well as specially designed equipment with high level of flexibility which enables quick adjustments of the future final products and possibility for fast changes in the parameters. This is why VEDA’s development process is characterized with efficiency and results, which can stand hand in hand with some of the most advances R&D centers around the world.

The company has established and operates a Quality Management System in accordance with ISO 9001:2015.
