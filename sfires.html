S-FireS

VEDA has devoted its R&D capacities and efforts towards developing a new technology with an early fire detections system which automatically ceases the fire within 5 seconds of fire-emergence. The result is VEDA’s very effective aerosol fire-extinguishing technology.

VEDA’s technology is small and convenient to use with a primary focus on fire-extinguishing in conditionally closed occupied or unoccupied areas. The fire extinguishing system consists of a solid state substance (result of our know-how), placed in a body of 100% recyclable aluminum. To achieve fast reaction, VEDA’s technology is programmed using artificial intelligence which can effectively detect the earliest forms of fire emergence.

The new fire extinguishing technology has the following characteristics:

  • Zero impact on the ozone layer and the local environment
  • Minimum to no impact on the technical equipment within the protected area
  • Dry system that will not damage paper archives, furniture or valuable arts during or after fire extinguishing
  • Extremely fast and efficient fire suppression, which prevents fire from spreading, thus helping to prevent the development of toxic gases during burning, the loss of human lives, fire damage, loss of goods etc.
